Moovly Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 21, 2025

Moovly respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

  • No Personal Information: Moovly does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII).
  • App Usage Data: We use Firebase Analytics to collect anonymous usage statistics to improve app performance.
  • Device Information: Basic device information may be collected for optimization purposes.
  • Preferences: Your app preferences (theme, language) are stored locally on your device.

2. How We Use Information

  • To provide and maintain the app service
  • To analyze app usage patterns for improvement
  • To fix bugs and enhance user experience
  • To display content from TMDB API

3. Data Storage & Security

  • Local Storage: All personal data is stored locally on your device.
  • Encryption: Sensitive data is encrypted when stored.
  • No Cloud Storage: We do not store your personal data on our servers.

4. Third-Party Services

  • TMDB API: We fetch movie/TV data from The Movie Database (TMDB). TMDB may collect information about API usage.
  • Firebase Analytics: Google's analytics service for anonymous usage statistics.
  • YouTube: Trailers are embedded from YouTube. YouTube's privacy policy applies.

5. Children's Privacy

Moovly is suitable for all ages but is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

6. Your Rights

  • You can access all data stored on your device
  • You can clear all app data through device settings
  • You can uninstall the app at any time

7. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy within the app.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

  • Email: moovly-privacy@byteland.app
  • or through our support channels within the app
